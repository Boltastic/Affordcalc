# 💸 Can I Afford This?

An AI-powered financial reality check web app.

Instead of just asking:
> “Can I buy this?”

This app answers:
> “Should you buy this?”

---

## 🚀 Features

### 💰 Affordability Calculator
Analyze purchases based on:
- income
- savings
- debt
- monthly expenses
- financial goals

---

### 🧠 AI Financial Advisor
Get realistic AI-generated financial advice:
- spending warnings
- saving suggestions
- cheaper alternatives
- long-term impact analysis

---

### ⏳ Real Cost Visualization
See purchases converted into:
- work hours
- months of savings
- percentage of income
- financial recovery time

Example:
> “This laptop costs 96 hours of your life.”

---

### 📈 Lifestyle Impact Simulator
Visualize:
- savings changes
- debt impact
- future affordability
- financial health over time

---

### ⚖️ Smart Comparison System
Compare:
- Buy now
- EMI
- Save first
- Alternative products

---

### 🎮 Gamified Finance
Includes:
- financial score
- savings streaks
- achievements
- risky spending alerts
